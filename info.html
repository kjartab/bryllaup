The venue

The wedding and reception will take place at Litsjeneset, an old farm turned vacation home. It is located in Jølster county, about a 30 minute drive from Førde, where Kjartan grew up. Renovating the farm has been quite the project and ongoing for 15 years. In 2014 and 2015 the barn was renovated and made ready for this wedding, a process which Helen also was part of.

Accommodation

We will provide you with accommodation either at the wedding venue or in Førde at the Bjørset's residence. If you would rather look into hotel options, there are several options in the area. The most convenient options would be Sunnfjord Hotel or Førde Hotel, both located in Førde.

To do around Jølster

Around Jølster there is plenty to do (if you like nature): River rafting, kayaking and hiking in an area with glaciers, mountains and fjords. If you would like to plan anything during your visit, please let us know and we will help you out. We plan on showing you parts of it both during and after the wedding.
