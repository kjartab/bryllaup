Map overview

Click on the markers to see more information about getting to "Sunnfjord", the area where Førde is and where the wedding will be. The best option for getting there is to fly in through Bergen and go by bus or car from there to Førde. A second option is to come in through Oslo, in which case you can take an 1-hour flight to Førde, where someone can pick you up.
